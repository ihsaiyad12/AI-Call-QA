# SalesGarners Design System

## Colors
- Primary: #6366F1 (Indigo)
- Success: #10B981 (Emerald)
- Warning: #F59E0B (Amber)
- Danger: #EF4444 (Rose)
- Background: #0F172A (Slate 900)
- Surface: rgba(30, 41, 59, 0.7) (Glassmorphism Slate 800)

## Typography
- Font Family: 'Inter', sans-serif
- Headings: Bold, clean, modern
- Body: Readable, well-spaced

## Components
- Cards: Glassmorphism effect, subtle border (rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.1)), 12px border radius.
- Buttons: Vibrant, high contrast, smooth transitions.
- Inputs: Dark themed, focused states with primary color glow.

## Aesthetic
- Modern, professional, AI-driven.
- Uses subtle gradients and micro-animations.
- High visual hierarchy for "Verdict" and "Scores".
